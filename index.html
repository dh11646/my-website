Formula 1

Formula 1 is a type of motorsport. Teams compete in a series of Grand Prix races, held in different countries around the world.
Some of the most popular races are held in Monaco, Singapore, Italy and Britain. An F1 car can reach up to 360 kmph during a race, and their cockpit can reach up to 50 degrees Celcius!
F1 cars are also the fastest racing machines in the world! Given that Formula 1 fans are attracted to
high-tech vehicles, speed, and driving skill, both McLaren, and Ferrari's history is home to the most iconic cars and the most legendary drivers
make it an enduring fan favorite.

Some of the most famous drivers that are known to be the best of the best include:

  • Michael Schumacher
  • Lewis Hamilton
  • Ayrton Senna

    And these are only a few of the many, many great drivers in Formula 1. The drivers that have been considered the greatest of all time, are these top 3.

    Michael Schumacher

    Since Michael Schumacher retired in 2012 he holds 7 world championships (tied with Lewis Hamilton), 91 wins, 155 podium finishes, and 68 pole positions.
    He was born January 3, 1969, Hürth-Hermülhein, West Germany (now in Germany). As a youth, Schumacher became interested in go-kart racing,
    this was supported by the fact that his father managed a go-kart track. In 1984 and 1985 he won the German junior karting championship, and in 1987 he had won
    the German and European karting titles. The year after, when he was at age 19, he retired from karting and became a Formula Three driver (these vehicles were/are less
    powerful than formula 1 cars). Two years later, in 1990, he had won the German F3 championship.
