Formula 1

Formula 1 is a type of motorsport. Teams compete in a series of Grand Prix races, held in different countries around the world. Some of the most popular races are held in Monaco, Singapore, Italy and Britain.
An F1 car can reach up to 360 kmph during a race, and their cockpit can reach up to 50 degrees Celcius! F1 cars are also the fastest racing machines in the world! Given that Formula 1 fans are attracted to
high-tech vehicles, speed, and driving skill, both McLaren, and Ferrari's history is home to the most iconic cars and the most legendary drivers make it an enduring fan favorite.
